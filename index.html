The University

THE TAMIL NADU DR. AMBEDKAR LAW UNIVERSITY WAS ESTABLISHED BY THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU TO ADVANCE AND DISSEMINATE KNOWLEDGE IN LEARNING, TEACHING, AND RESEARCH IN THE FIELD OF LAW. HIS EXCELLENCY SHRI KR. NARAYANAN, FORMER PRESIDENT OF INDIA INAUGURATED IT ON SEPTEMBER 20, 1997.

THE UNIVERSITY IS THE FIRST FULL-FLEDGED SUI GENERIS LAW UNIVERSITY THAT OFFERS LEGAL EDUCATION ONLY THROUGH THIS UNIVERSITY AND ITS AFFILIATED COLLEGES IN THE STATE AND THE UNIVERSITY IS THE FIRST OF ITS KIND NOT ONLY IN THE COUNTRY BUT ALSO IN SOUTH ASIA. THE UNIVERSITY OFFERS LEGAL EDUCATION AT ITS CAMPUS AS WELL AS THROUGH TWENTY-FOUR AFFILIATED COLLEGES IN THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU. THE UNIVERSITY OFFERS ACADEMIC PROGRAMS UNDER ITS SCHOOL OF EXCELLENCE IN LAW.

THE SCHOOL WAS ESTABLISHED ON THE LINES OF NATIONAL LAW UNIVERSITIES TO OFFER ALL ACADEMIC PROGRAMS. ITS THIRTEEN POST GRADUATE DEPARTMENTS WITH DIFFERENT SUBJECT SPECIALIZATIONS TAKE CARE OF LEGAL RESEARCH BY OFFERING DOCTORAL AND POST GRADUATE PROGRAMS. APART FROM THE DEPARTMENTS. THE SCHOOL ALSO OFFERS 5 UNDER GRADUATE PROGRAMS IN LAW WITH VARIED INTERDISCIPLINARY STREAMS OF 3 YEARS & 5 YEARS OF INTEGRATED LAW DEGREE COURSES, RESEARCH DEGREE (PH.D PROGRAM) AND LLD PROGRAM (DOCTORATE OF LAWS).
